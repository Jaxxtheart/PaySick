Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

Your Privacy Matters: PaySick is committed to protecting your personal and medical information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data in compliance with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you register with PaySick, we collect:

  • Full name and date of birth
  • South African ID number
  • Contact information (email, phone number, postal address)
  • Banking and payment details
  • Employment information (for creditworthiness assessment)

Medical Information

We collect limited medical information necessary to process your payment applications:

  • Healthcare provider details
  • Treatment dates and invoice amounts
  • Medical invoice details (but not detailed medical records)

Usage Information

We automatically collect:

  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and location data
  • Website usage and interaction data
  • Login and access times

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process your payment plan applications
  • Verify your identity and assess creditworthiness
  • Facilitate payments to healthcare providers
  • Communicate with you about your account and payments
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • Prevent fraud and unauthorized access

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We Share Information With:

  • Healthcare Providers: To confirm treatment and process payments
  • Credit Bureaus: For creditworthiness assessments (with your consent)
  • Payment Processors: To process your payments securely
  • Service Providers: Who help us operate our platform (under strict confidentiality)
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect our rights

We Never:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your medical details without authorization
  • Use your data for purposes other than stated in this policy

4. Data Security

We protect your information using:

  • Bank-level encryption (256-bit SSL/TLS)
  • Secure data storage with encrypted databases
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability testing
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Employee confidentiality agreements and training
  • Compliance with PCI DSS standards for payment data

5. Your Rights Under POPIA

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Object: Object to certain processing of your information
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw previously given consent
  • Complain: Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator

6. Data Retention

We retain your information:

  • For active accounts: Duration of your relationship with PaySick
  • For closed accounts: 7 years (as required by financial regulations)
  • Usage data: 2 years
  • Marketing preferences: Until you opt out

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to:

  • Remember your login status
  • Analyze website performance
  • Personalize your experience
  • Prevent fraud

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

8. Children's Privacy

PaySick is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe we have collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a prominent notice on our website. Continued use of PaySick after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
