PURPOSE OF THIS STATEMENT

This Accessibility Statement outlines PaySick (Pty) Ltd's ("PaySick") commitment to providing reasonable, inclusive, and equitable access to its digital services.

This Statement supports PaySick's broader commitments to:

  • Fair customer treatment;
  • Responsible platform conduct; and
  • Financial-services-grade governance standards.

1. ACCESSIBILITY COMMITMENT

PaySick is committed to making its websites, applications, and digital services accessible to as many users as reasonably possible, including individuals with disabilities.

Accessibility is approached in a manner that is:

  • Proportionate;
  • Risk-based;
  • Consistent with PaySick's business model and technical architecture.

2. ACCESSIBILITY STANDARDS

PaySick aims, where reasonably practicable, to align with:

  • The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA; and
  • Accessibility features supported by modern browsers, devices, and operating systems.

Design considerations include:

  • Adequate colour contrast and readable typography;
  • Keyboard navigation support;
  • Screen reader compatibility;
  • Clear instructions and error messaging.

3. LIMITATIONS AND THIRD-PARTY CONTENT

While PaySick makes reasonable efforts to support accessibility:

  • Certain third-party tools, integrations, or content may not fully conform to accessibility standards;
  • Accessibility may vary depending on user devices, browsers, and assistive technologies.

PaySick does not guarantee uninterrupted or error-free access across all assistive technologies.

4. ALTERNATIVE ACCESS AND SUPPORT

If you experience difficulty accessing any part of the PaySick Service, you may contact PaySick for assistance. Where reasonably feasible, alternative support or access methods will be provided.

5. GOVERNANCE AND REVIEW

Accessibility considerations form part of PaySick's:

  • Platform governance processes;
  • Risk management frameworks;
  • Periodic policy and control reviews.

This Statement may be updated from time to time to reflect platform changes or evolving best practices.

6. CONTACT DETAILS

For accessibility-related enquiries or support:

PaySick (Pty) Ltd

Email: support@paysick.co.za

Website: www.paysick.co.za

This Accessibility Statement is published to promote transparency, inclusion, and responsible digital service delivery.
