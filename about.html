Our Mission

At PaySick, we believe that financial barriers should never prevent anyone from receiving the healthcare they need. Our mission is to make medical care accessible to all South Africans by providing simple, transparent payment solutions that work for patients and healthcare providers alike.

We're building a future where everyone can access quality healthcare when they need it, without the stress of upfront costs or complicated financing.

Our Story

PaySick was founded in 2025 by a team of healthcare and financial technology professionals who experienced firsthand the challenges South Africans face when dealing with unexpected medical expenses.

After seeing too many people delay necessary medical care due to cost concerns, we decided to create a better solution. We partnered with healthcare providers and financial institutions to build a platform that works for everyone.

Today, PaySick is proud to be South Africa's leading healthcare payment platform, helping thousands of patients access the care they need while supporting healthcare providers in growing their practices.

Regulatory Compliance

PaySick operates under the strictest regulatory guidelines to protect our customers:

National Credit Act (NCA): We are a registered credit provider, ensuring all lending is conducted responsibly.

POPIA Compliance: Your personal information is protected in accordance with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act.

Financial Intelligence Centre Act (FICA): We maintain robust anti-money laundering and fraud prevention measures.
