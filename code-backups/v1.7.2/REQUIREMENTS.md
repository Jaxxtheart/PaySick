# Requirements & Specifications — PaySick v1.7.2

**Version**: 1.7.2
**Date**: 2026-04-15

---

## Changed Requirements

### Payment Plan Amount Limits

| ID | Requirement | Priority |
|----|-------------|----------|
| AMT-01 | The maximum payment plan amount selectable by a patient is R10,000 | Must Have |
| AMT-02 | The minimum payment plan amount selectable by a patient is R5,000 | Must Have |
| AMT-03 | Terms of Service section 4.3 must state the per-payment-plan maximum as R10,000 | Must Have |
