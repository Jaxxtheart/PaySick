Risk Management

Healthcare-specific underwriting edge

Our proprietary risk models are built specifically for healthcare financing - fundamentally different from retail BNPL.

Why Healthcare Lending is Different

  • Non-discretionary need: Patients finance essential procedures, not impulse purchases - creating higher repayment commitment
  • Higher ticket sizes: R18K avg. loan means fixed underwriting costs spread efficiently, attracting serious borrowers
  • Provider partnership: Healthcare providers become soft collection partners - patients return for follow-up care
  • Family support: Medical debt attracts family payment support unlike retail credit

Proprietary PD & LGD Models

Our healthcare-specific Probability of Default (PD) models incorporate:

  • Procedure type risk weighting
  • Provider network performance data
  • Healthcare-specific affordability ratios
  • Medical outcome correlation factors

Loss Given Default Mitigation

LGD optimization strategies unique to healthcare:

  • Provider-assisted soft collections
  • Employer garnish arrangements for formal sector
  • Medical aid assignment protocols
  • Family co-signer programs
